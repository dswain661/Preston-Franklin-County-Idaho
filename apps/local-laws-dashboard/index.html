Know before you act

  • County vs. city: Many Preston rules live in Franklin County code, especially land use and zoning near city boundaries.
  • Substantive rules (Rules + Enforcement) are what most likely affect property, business, and daily conduct.
  • Process sections explain permits, hearings, and how to appeal — critical if you disagree with a decision.
  • Official source: Always verify against the county clerk or official codification before relying on any summary.
